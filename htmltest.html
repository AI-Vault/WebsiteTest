AI Agents and Their Impact on Workflow and Society

\n

Introduction to AI Agents

\n

The podcast features Jelle Prins, an AI guru and co-founder of Cradle, discussing the revolutionary power of AI agents and their application in automating workflows. The conversation highlights the rising capabilities of AI agents compared to traditional AI models like chatbots, focusing on their ability to interact autonomously with different systems.
